Profile

Profile

I am pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.S. in Econometrics and Quantitative Economics at the University of South Florida's Genshaft Honors College, with a minor in Geographic Information Systems and a 4.0 GPA.

My work spans graph machine learning, spatial analysis, synthetic data generation, economic modeling, and financial technology. I have built software in Python, C++, R, C, C#, VBA, ReactJS, and tools including PyTorch/PyG, Pandas, Git, ArcGIS Pro, Terra, and ggplot2.
